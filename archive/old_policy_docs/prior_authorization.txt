Prior Authorization Requirements

Section 1: Procedures Requiring Prior Authorization
The following procedure categories require prior authorization before a claim will be considered for reimbursement: elective surgeries, advanced imaging (MRI, CT with contrast), and specialty pharmacy prescriptions exceeding a 30-day supply.

Section 2: Submission Timeline
Prior authorization requests must be submitted at least 5 business days before the scheduled procedure date. Requests submitted with less than 5 business days' notice may be processed on an expedited basis only if accompanied by a documented medical urgency justification.

Section 3: Denial and Appeal
If a prior authorization request is denied, the provider has 15 calendar days from the date of denial to submit a formal appeal, including any additional supporting clinical documentation.
