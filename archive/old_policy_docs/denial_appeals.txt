Claim Denial and Appeals Process

Section 1: Common Denial Reasons
Claims are commonly denied due to missing prior authorization, incomplete documentation, service not covered under the member's plan, or claims submitted past the timely filing deadline.

Section 2: Appeal Timeline
Providers or members may appeal a denied claim within 60 calendar days of the denial notice date. A first-level appeal is reviewed within 30 days of submission.

Section 3: Second-Level Appeal
If a first-level appeal is denied, a second-level appeal may be requested within 30 days of the first-level denial. Second-level appeals are reviewed by a separate clinical review panel and are typically resolved within 45 days.
